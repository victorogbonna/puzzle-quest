🧩 Puzzle Quest Adventure
📖 How to Play
🎯 Goal

Answer as many puzzles as you can to earn points and become a Puzzle Master!

🔤 Word Scramble

A word is jumbled up. Unscramble the letters to find the real word.

Example: TOBOR → ROBOT
🤔 Riddles

Read the clue carefully and type the one-word answer that fits.

Example: "I have hands but can't clap" → CLOCK
🔢 Math Puzzles

Solve the maths problem and type the number as your answer.

Example: 8 × 7 = ? → 56
⚙️ Controls
  • ENTER Submit your answer
  • 💡 HINT Get a clue — costs 1 point
  • ⏭️ SKIP Skip the question — costs 1 life ❤️
🏆 Scoring
  • ✅ Correct answer (no hint) → +10 points
  • ✅ Correct answer (with hint) → +5 points
  • ❌ Wrong answer → try again, no penalty
  • ⏭️ Skip → lose 1 life
❤️ Lives

You start with 3 lives. Lose all 3 and the game ends early. Good luck!
